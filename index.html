Hello.

I'm a PhD candidate in the Robotics Institute at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor. I work in the Lab for Progress with Prof. Chad Jenkins.

I studied Electrical Engineering at McGill University in Montreal, Canada, where I worked with the Mobile Robotics Lab and on McGill Robotics AUV design team. I also spent two summers at Clearpath's OTTO Motors division.
